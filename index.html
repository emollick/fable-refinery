Fable Refinery
A reconstruction inspired by SimRefinery, the lost 1992 Maxis Business Simulations training game built for the Chevron Richmond refinery. The plant behind this window is running the simulation.
Select a scenario.
Goal: maximize long-term profit, week to week. There is no ending. Click any unit for its controls; the left palette highlights any product stream on the plant, as in the original. You are fired at −$20M cash or after a second explosion.
Homage built from the historical record and surviving screenshots (the original software is lost). Not affiliated with Maxis or Chevron. Version 1.0, June 2026.
